What is the team size ?
The team size can range from 2 to 5 members.
Is there a provision for partial selection of members of a team ?
Can apply as an individual ? No, Hackathons are usually team events where everyone brings different point of views and solutions to the same table and brainstorm.
What if I don’t want to present my hack ?
Unfinished or unimpressive (so you think) projects should be presented anyway! Presenting your hack gives you a chance to be proud of what you’ve done, and a constructive perspective of where you should be headed next.
What if I don’t have a team or an idea ?
You can get coders and non-coders in your team. The size should be from 2 to 5. And well be helping you with ideas, check out the problem statements here.
What if I've never been to a hackathon before ?
Then we're so glad Bookingjini will be your first ever! It’s helpful to have some programming or technical experience, but it’s not a requirement. We’ll have talks, mentors and workshops to help you with your project.
Are there prizes for girl teams ?
Bookingjini has always been a huge supporter of gender equality and empowerment. We've treasured swag exclusively for girls to promote and encourage their participation.
Can I start working on my hack before the event ?
No. In the interest of fairness, students should not be working on their projects before Hack in the North begins and we do not allow participants to work on pre-existing projects. However, you can familiarize yourself with all the tools and technologies you intend to use beforehand!
