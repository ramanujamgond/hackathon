About the event

Hackathons provide a marvelous chance for coders as well as non-coders of all backgrounds to come together, and build something of utility. So whether you’re a student, business professional, or part-time programming enthusiast, BJHacks is for you. BookingJini is a hotel tech company that helps drive business to hotels through direct booking and deep-tech technologies. With the verge of fascinating advancements in the field of IoT, Artificial Intelligence and User Experience, we aim to upgrade our products to adapt.

This is where the hackathon comes in. During this time of the pandemic that has engulfed the world around us, only technology will be able to help us adapt out of it. We have tailor-made problem statements for the hackathon, check them out here. The hackathon will be conducted online and for any other queries, please head over to the FAQ section here. We believe, through technology and creative solutions, we can fight this pandemic and bring stability and up-gradation to the existing hotel-tech sector.
