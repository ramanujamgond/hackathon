Hurdles to Hop
Bookingjini is going to organise a 48 hours long hackathon with an aim to solve the problems of hoteliers as well as Travellers. The idea behind the concept Code and innovate while you isolate is to try and seek solutions to contain COVID-19 spread in the Hospitality Industry by automating the Hotel check in process. The problems are as follows.
Take Out the Human Interaction
After the Corona Virus situation normalises, people would be more cautious and we would like to help them by minimising their interaction with hotel staff or other humans inorder to prevent such diseases from spreading again. In the travel industry this can be avoidable through technology or a single App, where there is no intervention of humans. The problems are as follows.
  • The queue for check-in.
  • Carrying the luggage to my room.
  • Call the reception for any service like Laundry, room service, etc.
  • Call the restaurant and ask for the best offer for today.
  • Ask the service boy to set a wakeup call.
  • Manual Billing at the time of check-out.
  • Deposit the identity proof every time you stay.
  • Travel guide for nearby places.
  • Celebrations of customer special dates.
Get a Glimpse
Nowadays travelers are booking the room but most of the time they regret after entering into the room this is due to the difference in the photos of the room uploaded online and the actual look of the room. Even Reviews of hotels are not in real-time so its very difficult to find the real scenario of a hotel.
Smooth The Step In
An IoT device that can detect mobile app for checking into the hotel. Usually hotels provide their customers with a key to their room, this step can be improvised in a way such that there would be no need for a key. Instead the door would open just by detecting the presence of the user's mobile in the vicinity. Hence reducing friction in turn enhancing User Experience and smooth check-in.
